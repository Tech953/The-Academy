## 1. **Cognition**
**What it really measures:**  
Pattern recognition, abstraction, compression of meaning, epistemic confidence.

**High Cognition characters:**
- NPCs speak in incomplete thoughts, conditional logic, and implication
- Explanations are *withheld* or abbreviated
- Dialogue assumes shared premises

**Low Cognition characters:**
- NPCs overexplain, repeat, or simplify
- Dialogue becomes linear and procedural
- NPCs may redirect or exclude the player from deeper conversations

**Tonal Shift Example:**
> Low: "This room tests memory. You must repeat the sequence exactly."  
> High: "You already see the pattern. Don't insult it by rehearsing."

---

## 2. **Presence**
**What it really measures:**  
Social gravity, existential weight, how much reality bends around you.

**High Presence characters:**
- NPCs pause before speaking
- Titles, rituals, and formalities emerge
- Descriptions frame the player as *causal*

**Low Presence characters:**
- NPCs interrupt, correct, or talk past you
- The player is framed as *incidental*
- Authority ignores or undermines you

**Tonal Shift Example:**
> Low: "Stand there. You're in the way."  
> High: "If you intend to remain, we will proceed accordingly."

---

## 3. **Resonance**
**What it really measures:**  
Attunement to symbolic systems, metaphysical coherence, myth-literacy.

**High Resonance characters:**
- NPCs speak in layered metaphor that still feels *precise*
- Symbols clarify instead of obscure
- Ritual language becomes actionable

**Low Resonance characters:**
- Symbolism feels evasive or alienating
- NPCs default to literal or dismissive explanations
- Mystical NPCs may shut down entirely

**Tonal Shift Example:**
> Low: "It's just a bell. Ring it when told."  
> High: "You know why it rings before it does."

---

## 4. **Fortitude**
**What it really measures:**  
Psychological endurance, resistance to coercion, capacity to remain intact.

**High Fortitude characters:**
- NPCs abandon intimidation and manipulation
- Hard truths are delivered directly-or withheld out of respect
- Threats lose performative flourish

**Low Fortitude characters:**
- NPCs probe, test, destabilize
- Dialogue includes pressure, bait, or soft cruelty
- Descriptions emphasize vulnerability

**Tonal Shift Example:**
> Low: "Let's see how long you last."  
> High: "There's nothing here that wants to break you."

---

## 5. **Expression**
**What it really measures:**  
Aesthetic fluency, rhythm, creative synthesis, emotional articulation.

**High Expression characters:**
- NPCs use cadence, imagery, metaphor, and parallelism
- Dialogue feels *performed*, not spoken
- The world is described musically or texturally

**Low Expression characters:**
- Language becomes blunt, technical, or dry
- NPCs avoid abstraction
- Descriptions flatten

**Tonal Shift Example:**
> Low: "The mechanism is damaged."  
> High: "It's out of tune. Hear that drag in the second movement?"

---

## 6. **Faith**
**What it really measures:**  
Commitment to belief, trust in unseen systems, willingness to act without proof.

**High Faith characters:**
- NPCs speak as if outcomes are *already in motion*
- Language implies destiny, convergence, or obligation
- Uncertainty is treated as fuel, not flaw

**Low Faith characters:**
- NPCs hedge, qualify, and defer
- Dialogue emphasizes risk, doubt, and contingency
- Mystical certainty is treated with suspicion

**Tonal Shift Example:**
> Low: "This might work. No guarantees."  
> High: "It will happen. The question is whether you're present for it."
